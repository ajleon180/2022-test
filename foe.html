Dandelions are Foes!

Invasive Species

Due to their resilience, ability to grow almost anywhere, and the speed at which they spread and grow, dandelions are considered by many to be a weed. Dandelions are often found in places where they were not intentionally planted, including lawns, landscaped planters, and gaps in pavement.

Allergies

Being a flower, dandelions produce pollen. This pollen is a common allergen which causes countless people throughout the world seasonal misery.
